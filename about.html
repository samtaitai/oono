Hello :)

Nice to meet you!

I am a college student studying computer programming at Seneca College. I love learning and all kinds of challenges that I run into while I'm learning; I also cherish a good quality time spending with my favorite ones.

Playing UNO is one of my fond memories I had with my friends back in the time in Korea, the ones I haven't seen for years so I really miss them and those times we spent together! It was great having an opportunity to revisit my favorite card game while I'm showing my skills of front-end programming languages.

Soyon Lee

Passionate junior developer
